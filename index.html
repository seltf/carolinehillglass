Hi, I’m Caroline and welcome to my website. I work with fused glass to create unique and individual pieces including jewellery and decorative items. My designs are composed of glass elements each cut by hand then fused in a kiln at temperatures in excess of 1000 °F . The result is a unique piece of artwork with an extraordinary play of light that can only be fully appreciated first-hand.
I hope you enjoy browsing the website and please contact me if you would like further details or have any questions regarding my work.
