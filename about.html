After completing an Art foundation year, I spent a further two years at Cornwall College of Art and Design where I gained a DATEC Higher Diploma in Ceramics.

In 1986 I moved to Germany where I pursued my love of watercolours. I exhibited paintings in Germany and Belgium, which were mainly studies of flowers and wildlife. I returned to the UK and eventually settled in the Vale of Glamorgan in 1992, just a short walk from the beautiful scenery of the Welsh coast.

An interest in exploring new mediums led to further studies but it was my introduction to fused glass which captivated me. It seemed a natural progression from my roots in ceramics and I was immediately hooked. Inspired by the colours and reflective qualities of the glass, it is always an exciting moment to see what comes out of the kiln. I use premium materials which enable me to create unique pieces with an extraordinary play of light.
